Whitewater Rapids

At Whitewater Rapids, our mission is to deliver safe, exhilarating, and memorable river adventures for families and thrill-seekers alike. Our experienced guides, modern equipment, and passion for rivers ensure you leave with a smile and an unforgettable story.

History

Founded in 2005 by experienced river guides, Whitewater Rapids began as a small local outfitter and has since grown into a trusted regional company known for safety, professionalism, and fun.
